# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and learners of the **#10WeeksOfAWS** community pledge to make participation a harassment-free, welcoming, and encouraging experience for everyone — regardless of experience level, background, identity, or where you are in your cloud journey.

We're all here to learn AWS and grow together.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps our community thrive:

- Being respectful, patient, and supportive of learners at every level
- Giving constructive, kind feedback
- Celebrating others' progress and learn-in-public posts
- Asking questions freely — no question is "too basic"
- Correcting mistakes gracefully and citing sources

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, discriminatory or demeaning comments
- Trolling, personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Spamming, self-promotion unrelated to the challenge, or gatekeeping
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional learning space

## Our Responsibilities

Community maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and will take fair, appropriate action in response to any unacceptable behavior — including removing comments, posts, or contributions, and, where necessary, banning offenders.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces — this repository, its issues and PRs, and public spaces where someone is representing the #10WeeksOfAWS community (LinkedIn, X, GitHub, Discord/Telegram, live sessions).

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community maintainers via **TrainWithShubham** and **CloudAdhar** official channels ([trainwithshubham.com](https://trainwithshubham.com) · [trainwithcloudadhar.com](https://trainwithcloudadhar.com)). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
